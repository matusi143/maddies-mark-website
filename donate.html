Free or Low-Cost Fundraising Alternatives

We encourage exploring platforms with lower or zero fees to maximize impact:

  • Zeffy: 100% free fundraising with no platform fees, ensuring every penny goes to the cause.
  • Give Lively: Free platform for non-profits, powered by tech to simplify giving without costs.
  • 99Pledges: No startup or platform fees, ideal for peer-to-peer campaigns.
  • Donorbox: Free signup with low processing fees, customizable forms for easy donations.

These options are better than high-fee services like PayPal because they reduce overhead, allowing more funds for programs like Best Days Ever.
