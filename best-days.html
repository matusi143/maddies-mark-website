Best Days Ever

A ‘Best Day Ever’ is an opportunity for families to create lasting memories during tough times, like visiting special places or enjoying unique activities.

Our Philosophy

Inspired by Maddie, we help relieve stress and cherish time together for children 18 and under in New York State facing serious challenges.

Examples of Impact

  • Naomi's VIP Phillies game and museum visits amid her cancer battle.
  • Davis's family camping and safari adventures post-tumor diagnosis.
  • Myles's reptile party and favorite treats for joyful moments.
  • Julian's Hershey Park trip while fighting Neuroblastoma.
  • Devon's superhero dinner celebration with custom pins.

To nominate a child, email BDE@MaddiesMark.org. Families in need, we're here to help make your best day happen.
